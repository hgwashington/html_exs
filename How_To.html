How to Breathe

Our unique breathing technique was developed in our labs in a undisclosed location. After countless lab mice experiments and several human trials we have developed a 7 step process that is guaranteed to get anyone, regarless of age, sex, or religion, breathing in a matter of days... if you're serious and follow our steps, completely.

7 Steps:

  1. Managing Expectations

    Talk to your doctor to make sure breathing is right for you. Certain conditions can make breathing impossible.

  2. Positive Mindset

    Once you have dicussed your new lifestyle with your healthcare provider, take some time to do things that make you happy. Remember it will be easier for you to commit to the process if you have a positive attitude.

  3. Support System

    Let your friends and family know that you plan to start breathing. Express to them that you would like to have their support, going foward.

  4. Environmental Control

    Before you begin make sure you are in an environment without airway restiction. Most places should be fine. Try to avoid being underwater or buried alive.

  5. Know your "Why"

    Keep in mind why you're doing this. Why did you start breathing in the first place? Is it because you have to or do you have a greater purpose?

  6. Find your Center

    Now, that you have your mind right, sit down (or stand). Ready your lungs! Here we go!

  7. Actualize

    If you have followed steps one through six, there is know way that you have not taken at least one breath. Congradulations!
